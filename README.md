1. Scroll to top of page, and click **Code**

3. Extract ZIP onto computer.
   
## üöÄ How to Use

1.  **Open the App**: Double-click `HellPostcardPrinter.exe` (Windows) or open `index.html` in any web browser.
2.  **Import Codes**: Click the green **üìÇ Import** button in the sidebar and select your Excel/CSV file containing the unique codes.
3.  **Select Column**: Choose the column name that contains your voucher codes (e.g., "Web Code").
4.  **Align Fields**: 
    *   Drag the red text labels (Offer, Code, Expiry, etc.) to sit exactly on the lines of your template.
    *   If you need more fields, click **+ Field**.
5.  **Save Your Layout**: Click **Save As...** and give your layout a name (e.g., "Store 666 Layout"). This saves your specific printer calibration to your browser's memory.
6.  **Print**: Click **Generate & Print Vouchers**. Your system's print dialog will open automatically.

## üñ®Ô∏è Printer Calibration Tip
Every printer has slightly different margins. If the text is printing a few millimeters off:
- Nudge the fields in the app to compensate.
- If it prints too far left, move the label further right in the app.
- Save your named template again to lock in those settings.
